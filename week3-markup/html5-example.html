Kimiko Ishizaka - J.S. Bach, The Art of Fugue, BWV 1080 - 01 Contrapunctus 1

A recording by pianist Kimiko Ishizaka of J.S. Bach's Art of Fuge, or Die Kunst der Fuge. The date of the piece is unclear but the earliest known manuscript is from 1740, when Bach lived in Leipzig, Germany. The audio file was downloaded from Wikimedia Commons.
